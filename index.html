About

I’m a backend developer and computer science educator based in Kathmandu, Nepal. For the last four years I’ve split my time between two things I care equally about: shipping software and teaching people how to build it.

On the development side I work mostly in Python and Django REST Framework, with earlier experience in Android (Java, Cordova) and a solid foundation in C/C++ and Java. On the teaching side I lecture undergraduate programming at Islington College, teach Grades 11–12 Computer Science at Kavya College, and author chapters, video scripts, and teacher-training materials for Ing Impact and the national CS curriculum.

I’m open to freelance and contract work — particularly Python/Django backend projects, REST API design, and technical content development. If you’re building something in that space, let’s talk.
